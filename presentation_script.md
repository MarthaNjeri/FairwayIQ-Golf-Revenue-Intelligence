# 🎤 FairwayIQ Board Presentation Script

## Opening

Good morning,

Today I’ll walk you through FairwayIQ — a data-driven view of the club’s performance, focusing on revenue, member engagement, and early indicators of churn risk.

The goal is to support better decision-making around retention, member value, and overall club profitability.

---

## 📊 Executive Overview

At a high level, the club is performing steadily, with revenue largely driven by highly engaged members.

However, early signs of disengagement are emerging in certain segments, which could impact future revenue if not addressed.

**Key Insight:**
Engagement is directly tied to revenue — members who play more contribute significantly more financially.

---

## 👥 Members (Value & Composition)

This view focuses on member composition, value, and lifecycle.

**Insight:**
Long-term members tend to generate higher value, making their retention especially important.

---

## 📈 Engagement

This section highlights how actively members interact with the club.

**Key Insight:**
Engagement is the strongest predictor of both revenue and retention.

Members with low activity are significantly more likely to become at-risk.

---

## 🏌️ Play Activity

This page analyzes on-course behavior, including course usage and tee time trends.

**Insight:**
There are opportunities to improve utilization during low-activity periods through targeted engagement strategies.

---

## ⚠️ Retention & Churn Risk

This is the most critical section, identifying members at risk before churn occurs.

**Key Insight:**
Churn is not random — it is typically preceded by declining engagement, especially among mid- to high-value members.

This creates an opportunity for early intervention.

---

## 🎯 Closing

In summary, engagement is the key driver of both revenue and retention.

By identifying at-risk members early and improving engagement, the club can protect revenue and enhance long-term member value.

Thank you. I’m happy to explore specific strategies based on these insights.
